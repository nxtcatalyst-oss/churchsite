02 / Hospitality

New here?

We know visiting a new church can be intimidating. We've gathered everything you need to know to feel at home before you even walk through the doors.

Get Directions

Parking & Arrival

Guest parking is available directly in front. Look for the "New Here" signs—our greeting team is ready to welcome you.

For Your Kids

Safe, fun, and age-appropriate environments for ages 0-12. Check-in opens 15 minutes before service for your peace of mind.

What to Wear

We're casual here. Jeans and a t-shirt are totally fine. Come as you are, comfortably, and be yourself.

How long is the service?

Our main service lasts about 75 minutes, featuring contemporary worship, prayer, and a practical message.

What is the worship like?

We range from energetic contemporary songs to reflective hymns, all centered on praising God and building community.
