Our Story

Welcome to the
Gathering.

We are a community centered on the life and teachings of Jesus, seeking to bring hope and restoration to Coeur d'Alene.

Our Mission

To create a space where everyone is welcome, where faith is authentic, and where the love of God is practically demonstrated in our city.

Authentic Faith

Living out our beliefs with honesty, transparency, and grace.

Foundational truths that guide our path and shape our interactions with the world around us.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired word of God, providing a reliable guide for living a full and meaningful life.
